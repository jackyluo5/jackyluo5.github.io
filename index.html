About me!

Hi my name is Jacky Luo and I am a Junior at the Unversity of California Irvine studying Computer Science. I fell into this major because I'm passionate about technology and how things come to be. I am passionate about coding and embedded systems which leads me to me spending most of time trying to learn new languages and try to apply them to personal projects. I really love spending my time watching Youtube videos about technology and why things work the way they do.

Somethings that inspire me to pursue Computer Science and the STEM industry is the innovations and impacts of NASA and how far technology has come. I am passionate about embedded systems where software meets hardware, and how it's able to create new technology and products. I am interested in understanding the process of this development as I try to build my software skills through coding projects and technical skills by working with robots! I want to continue learning about different processes of design and implementation of products as it is one of the most important steps of developing new technology. I like to challenge myself with new projects as I am restoring a 30 year old car trying to learn about what it takes to run a mechanical engine.
